Infinite Drums (Free)
---------------------

Hello Unity Devs!
This is a "free" version of a new Audio Asset dedicated to Drums!
The full pack includes interactive/adaptive and randomly generated soundtracks from a variety of samples in three different sytles: epic/orchestral, ethnic/world and solo drums.

All tracks are in MP3/OGG format, 192kb/s quality.

In the meantime, you can already enjoy this free pack which includes standalone extracts from all of the pieces included inside my upcoming asset! 
Hope you'll enjoy them as much as I enjoyed composing them!
Let me know what you think!
